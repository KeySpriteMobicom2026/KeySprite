#### 🔍 Project Overview

What is StretchQuest?  
A lightweight Unity-based module that integrates with school-managed devices (e.g., via Apple School Manager or Google Workspace for Education). It triggers short posture-awareness games at set intervals (e.g., after 30–45 minutes of continuous use), encouraging students to:

- Take visual breaks  
- Perform basic neck stretches  
- Stay physically aware while using screens  

Why this matters:  
Studies show prolonged screen use can lead to poor posture, eye strain, and reduced physical activity in students. Our module aims to gently introduce movement and awareness without interrupting learning flow.
