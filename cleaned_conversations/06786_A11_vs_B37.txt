>

### 3. Cultural Sensitivity（文化敏感度）
> 

| Indicator | Description |
|----------|-------------|
| 👍 Context Awareness | Does the message adjust for high-context vs. low-context norms? |

| 👍 Symbolic Meaning | Are certain words, phrases, or symbols (like emoji) interpreted differently across cultures? |

### 4. Medium Appropriateness（媒介适配）
> 

| Indicator | Description |
|----------|-------------|
| 👍 Content Fit | Is the message too complex for IM or too casual for email? |
| 👍 Urgency Match | Did I choose the right channel for how fast this needs to be handled? |
| 👍 Format Expectation | Does the structure follow expected norms for this medium (e.g., subject line in email, thread etiquette in IM)? |

🧠 引导问题：  
- Could this conversation have been better handled with a quick call or meeting instead?

|----------|-------------|
